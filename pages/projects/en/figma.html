Figma for Frontend Developers

The task of the webinar is to introduce students to one of the most popular modern online editors for web designers and frontend developers, which is replacing Photoshop.

This webinar is designed for the most beginners who are just starting to learn the layout.

Students will learn about the state of the editor market for web design, their place in workflow, the pros and cons of online editors compared to Photoshop. The main part of the webinar is the analysis of the basic tasks of frontend developer working with templates.

Plan

  • Sizes
  • Distances
  • Colors
  • Fonts
  • Content

The webinar demonstrates work with different templates, including my own and figma built-ins. But the main one could be a layout from your educational org.

At the end there is QnA as usual.
