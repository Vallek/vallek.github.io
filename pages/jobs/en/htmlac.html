Work at HTML Academy

Tutor (2019-2021)

Consulted students via voice and text, checked homework practice and diploma, worked with git/GitHub.

Achievements

At the Academy, more than in other places, you can help a particular student personally. This allows not only to explain the solution of the problem, which is often impossible in a group classes, but also to support student and point them in the right direction, try to help with solving learning problems.

What I learned on this job

The Academy employs some of the most competent and modern front-end teachers that I have seen. You can learn a lot from them. Especially when it comes to best practices and the development of criteria for evaluating works.
